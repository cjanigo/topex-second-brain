## Usage Notes

- **When to use this template:** Any project combining topo/site survey with civil site design. Typical examples: park improvements, utility corridors, stormwater facilities, site grading plans, ADA upgrades.
- **Task 1 (PM) is always included.** Copy the boilerplate language verbatim.
- **Task 5 (Reimbursables) is always included.** Itemize survey equipment and technology fees separately — clients notice.
- **Survey Equipment Fee:** $250/day is standard. Estimate days needed and include that in the fee.
- **Technology Fee:** 2% of direct labor costs is standard.
- **T&M vs Fixed Fee:** Use T&M (do-not-exceed) when design scope has unknowns (agency feedback, utility conflicts, equipment layout changes). Use Fixed Fee only when the design is fully defined at scope time.
- **Construction coordination (Task 4):** Include only if the client wants Topex to lead or support bidding. Some clients handle this themselves.
- **Slide/equipment layout:** Always note that footprint dimensions are per manufacturer specs — this limits your liability if the equipment design changes.
- **ESC plan:** Include for any project that disturbs soil. Even a playground counts.
- **Attachment A:** Always attach Topex General Terms and Conditions.
