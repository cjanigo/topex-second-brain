## Overview

Chris is an active member of the Professional Land Surveyors of Oregon (PLSO). PLSO is the primary professional association for licensed land surveyors in Oregon. Membership provides CE resources, legislative advocacy, networking, and chapter events. PLSO partners with LSAW (Washington) and ISPLS (Idaho) via nwsurveyor.com.
