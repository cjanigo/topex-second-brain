# Work & Business — Topex Inc.

**Full Name:** Topex Inc. Professional Engineering and Land Surveying
**Structure:** Sole proprietor / solo operator

## Services & Revenue Streams

| Service | Description | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Civil Engineering | Engineered plans, hydraulic models, city engineering roles, reports | Highest revenue |
| Land Surveying | Survey plats, records of survey, boundary work, field surveys | Most time-consuming, biggest backlog |
| Water Rights | Water rights examinations and consulting | Specialized credential |
| Consultations | Expert witness, technical reports, client advising | High value, time-intensive |
| Erosion & Sediment Control | CESCL-level design and consulting | As needed |
| UAS / Drone Services | Survey-grade aerial data collection | Supports surveying and engineering |

## Key Tension
Engineering generates the most revenue. Surveying takes the most time and has the biggest backlog. Prioritize clearing the surveying backlog to protect capacity for higher-revenue engineering work.
