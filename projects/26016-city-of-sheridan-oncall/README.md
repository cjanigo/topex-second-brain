On-call engineering and land surveying support for day-to-day City operations. Scope per SOW signed 2026-02-15.

- Task 1: Project management and administrative services
- Task 2: Transportation services (ADA, TMP coordination, construction inspection, ROW permits, street improvements)
- Task 3: Stormwater management (master plan, MS4 compliance, COG coordination)
- Task 4: Water treatment and distribution (OHA compliance letter, hydraulic modeling)
- Task 5: Wastewater collections and treatment (design and operational support)
- Task 6: Private development review (pre-app meetings, plan review, easement review)
