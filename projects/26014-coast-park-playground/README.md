## Project Description

Survey and civil site design for a new playground at Coast Park, located at SW Coast St between SW 2nd Street and W Olive St in Newport, Oregon. The City contracted a playground equipment supplier to design new play equipment (slide and other apparatus). Topex is providing the topographic survey, base map, and civil design to support equipment installation including ADA access, grading, surface cover, landscaping, frontage improvements on SW Dolphin St., and an interim erosion and sediment control plan.
