## Background

OWD and Netarts Water District want to create an intertie connection near the Capes Tank, where both distribution systems are in close proximity. OWD received a $20,000 grant from Business Oregon's Water/Wastewater Financing Program to fund this PER.

## Scope of Work

Per CWES Engineering Scope of Services (Attachment A):

**Task 1 - Project Management:** Coordination with both districts, including a meeting to review the draft PER.

**Task 2 - Preliminary Engineering Report:**
- Kick-off, site visit, and data collection from both districts
- Data needed from districts: distribution maps, tank locations/elevations, pump stations, pipeline layouts, easements and land information
- Analysis and evaluation of intertie options
- Recommendation of preferred intertie structure(s)
- Schematic site layout of proposed intertie location
- Summary of land situation for proposed location
- Project cost estimate
- Funding options

**Task 3 - Reimbursables:** Mileage, per diem, housing, copies, etc.

**Not Included:** Computer water modeling, seismic risk assessment, field surveying, public involvement
