# SOP: Writing a Survey Scope of Work

## When to Use This

Any time a client requests a boundary survey, legal description, record of survey, or easement work. Use `templates/survey-sow.md` as the starting document.

---

## Step 1 — Gather Information Before Writing

You need these before the SOW can be written:

| Item | Where to Get It |
|---|---|
| Client name and address | Email or phone inquiry |
| Tax lot number(s) | County assessor / client |
| Recording instrument number | County deed records or client |
| Property owner name (if different from client) | County records |
| What they want done | Client description |
| Site access confirmed | Client |

---

## Step 2 — Define the Tasks

Most survey scopes are 2 tasks:

- **Task 1 – Field Work:** One-day field survey, staking, monumentation recovery
- **Task 2 – Office Deliverables:** Legal descriptions, record of survey, easement exhibit, or plat

Add a Task 3 only if there is a recording or agency submittal step (e.g., county recording of a plat).

---

## Step 3 — Price It

Standard fee ranges (update as rates change):

| Service | Typical Range |
|---|---|
| One-day field survey (staking/monumentation) | $950 to $1,250 |
| Legal description (per parcel) | $250 to $400 |
| Easement legal description + exhibit | $300 to $500 |
| Record of Survey (full) | $1,500 to $2,500 |
| Add credit card fee if applicable | 3% of subtotal |

---

## Step 4 — Payment Terms

Standard: 50% down before scheduling, 50% before final deliverables.

- Down payment secures the field date
- Final payment is a hard stop before releasing legal descriptions or exhibits
- For municipalities or repeat accounts, net-30 billing is acceptable

---

## Step 5 — Schedule Language

In Part D, describe the schedule in relative terms (do not commit to exact dates in the SOW document):

> "We are prepared to begin work during [time window] and deliver final documents within a week of field work."

Exact dates are scheduled separately once the signed scope and down payment are received.

---

## Step 6 — Send and File

1. Send SOW as PDF via email
2. Request signed copy returned via email
3. Create project folder in `projects/` with README
4. Note down payment status in README on receipt

---

## Key Rules

- Never schedule field work until down payment is received
- Never release Task 2 deliverables until final payment is received
- Always attach General Terms and Conditions (Attachment A)
- Exclusions list must be present in every SOW — add project-specific exclusions as needed
