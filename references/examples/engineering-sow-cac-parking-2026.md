# Example: Engineering SOW - CAC Parking Lot Analysis (2026)

_Signed scope of work for Children's Advocacy Center of Lincoln County, parking alternatives analysis. Good example of a desktop-only civil engineering study with no field work, fixed fee, and multi-agency coordination._

---

**Client:** Paul Schrader, Executive Director
**Organization:** Children's Advocacy Center of Lincoln County
**Date:** March 9, 2026
**Signed:** March 10, 2026
**Fee:** $10,000 Fixed Fee

---

## Overview

The CAC has a shortfall of parking spaces and needs additional parking to meet current needs and projected buildout. The property at 125 NE 47th Street, Newport, OR is owned by the City of Newport. Work includes a desktop site analysis to evaluate potential parking layouts considering topography, site constraints, ADA requirements, and municipal code requirements. Several alternatives will be prepared with +/-30% cost estimates, summarized in a report. Upon CAC approval, the report will be shared with the City of Newport for feedback.

## Scope of Work

**Task 1 - Project Management and Administrative Services**
Processing paperwork and correspondence, coordination on financial matters, tracking schedule and budget, reviewing progress.

**Task 2 - Parking Alternatives Analysis**
Confer with client on future growth projections. Evaluate parking layouts considering topography, site constraints, ADA, and municipal code. No topographic survey - use DOGAMI LIDAR data for basemap. Develop several alternative parking concept plans for existing and future needs.

**Task 3 - Alternatives Report**
Develop 30% +/- cost estimates, complete the alternatives analysis report, and coordinate with the City of Newport to review the alternatives.

**Task 4 - Reimbursable**
Technology, reproduction, postage, shipping, travel (mileage, per diem, lodging), and other reimbursable costs.

## Fee

Fixed Fee: $10,000. Invoiced monthly based on hours worked. Invoices show single line item "Engineering Services" - no task differentiation. Amendments required for additional services before work begins.

## Payment Terms

Due upon receipt. 9% monthly late charge after 30 days.

## Key Features of This SOW

- Desktop-only study (no field survey) using DOGAMI LIDAR as basemap
- Fixed fee with monthly progress invoicing
- Two-phase client coordination: first CAC review, then City of Newport
- ADA and municipal code compliance built into scope
- 30% cost estimates as a deliverable (not a full design)
- Report includes "next steps" to frame the path to implementation
