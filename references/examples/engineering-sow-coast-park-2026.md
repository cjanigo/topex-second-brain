## Overview

The City of Newport contracted a playground equipment supplier to design and install a new slide and play equipment at Coast Park (SW Coast St between SW 2nd St and W Olive St, Newport, OR). To support installation, Topex was engaged to provide a topographic survey, civil site design, ADA access routing, grading plan, landscaping plan, ESC plan, and full construction documents including a cost estimate. Bidding support and construction-phase observation were also included.

## Scope of Work

**Task 1 - Project Management and Administration ($1,740)**
One site visit with City staff and advisory committee. Online meetings and phone calls. Financial coordination and schedule tracking.

**Task 2 - Field Survey and Base Map ($2,752)**
Field survey of Coast Park including all features, structures, grades, and site conditions. Base map of existing area created for design use.

**Task 3 - Design, Contract/Construction Documents and Cost Estimate ($11,545)**
Full civil site design including:
- ADA access routes through the playground area
- Footprint locations for the slide (per manufacturer specs)
- Surrounding play area containment with access and borders
- Other play equipment locations
- Full impacted area surface cover and landscaping plan
- Frontage improvements on SW Dolphin St.
- Interim Erosion and Sediment Control Plan

Construction documents prepared to permit-ready standards. Construction cost estimate included.

**Task 4 - Bidding and Construction Coordination ($2,836)**
Bid solicitation support. RFI responses and submittal review. Two (2) site visits: one during construction, one final inspection.

**Task 5 - Reimbursables ($250 + $250 survey equipment + $382 technology = $882 total reimbursables)**
Mileage, survey equipment ($250/day), technology charge (2% of direct labor), reproduction, shipping.

## Work Not Included

- Boundary surveys, easements, permissions
- City utility design
- Construction staking (contractor provides; Topex sets control points)
- County permit approval and fees
- 1200-C Construction Stormwater support
- Bid advertisement costs
- One-year warranty inspection
- Geotechnical investigation

## Key Features of This SOW

- Combined survey and civil design scope in one contract — avoids separate survey agreement
- T&M do-not-exceed: monthly invoicing up to the cap, no lump-sum risk
- ADA and ESC plan both included within design task — common public project requirement
- Equipment layout explicitly tied to manufacturer specs — limits liability if layout changes
- Two construction site visits scoped and priced (not open-ended construction admin)
- Bidding support included — useful for public-sector clients who may need help soliciting contractors
- All agency permit fees excluded — standard language, prevents surprise cost disputes
- Contractor responsible for construction staking — Topex provides control points only
