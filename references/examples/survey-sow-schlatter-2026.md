# Example: Survey SOW — Schlatter Fernview Lane (March 2026)

**Project:** 26008 - Jim Schlatter - SW Fernview Lane, Lincoln County, OR
**Date:** March 5, 2026
**Total Fee:** $1,853
**Services:** Boundary survey stakeout + legal descriptions + easement exhibit

---

## What Made This Scope Work Well

- Two-task structure kept it simple: field work ($1,150) and office work ($650)
- 50/50 payment split tied to task delivery — protects against non-payment before final deliverables
- Easement scope was explicit: north parcel dedicated to south parcel, access rights only
- Deliverables listed exact counts: two (2) legal descriptions + one (1) easement legal description with exhibit on 8.5x11

## Fee Breakdown Reference

| Task | Description | Fee |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Field work (one day, staking + monumentation) | $1,150 |
| 2 | Legal descriptions (x2) + easement exhibit | $650 |
| | Credit card fee (3%) | $53 |
| | **Total** | **$1,853** |

## Scope Notes

- Two tax lots on same road, client wants to sell independently — needed separate legal descriptions per parcel
- Access easement required because north parcel landlocks south parcel
- One-day field estimate was appropriate given lot size and site conditions
- Final deliverable: 8.5x11 exhibit sheet — keep it simple for Lincoln County recording

## Payment Notes

- $900 down payment received at signing (slightly rounds down from true 50%)
- Final $953 due before releasing Task 2 deliverables
- Client paid by credit card — hence the 3% fee line item
